
# Day 001 - Even or Odd Number Checker

Topic      : Numbers, Conditionals

Difficulty : Easy

Date       : 04-06-2026

-------------------------------------------
## PROBLEM:
-------------------------------------------
Given a number, check whether it is Even or Odd.

- Even numbers are divisible by 2 (remainder = 0)
  Example: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10

- Odd numbers are NOT divisible by 2 (remainder = 1)
  Example: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9

-------------------------------------------
## CONCEPT USED:
-------------------------------------------
Modulus operator  →  %
It gives the REMAINDER after division.

  10 % 2 = 0   → Even
   7 % 2 = 1   → Odd

-------------------------------------------
## EXPLANATION (Step by Step):
-------------------------------------------
Step 1: Take a number as input from the user

Step 2: Divide it by 2 and check the remainder

Step 3: If remainder is 0 → Even
        If remainder is 1 → Odd
        
Step 4: Print the result
